For Mejasan's crew – Morning Interview Guide This guide is used on the morning of the wedding. Share this page with your client the evening before — they read and reflect on the questions privately, then speak from the heart during the filmed interview. No written answers needed.

Introduction

01Please introduce yourself — what is your full name?
02What is today's date?
03What is happening today?

About Your Partner

01Who are you marrying today?
02In a few words, how would you describe them?
03What is the best thing about them?
04What quality made you certain they are the one?

Expectations for the Day

01How are you feeling this morning?
02What are your expectations for today?
03What would make today perfect for you?

The Moment You Are Waiting For

01What moment are you most looking forward to?
02Why is that moment special to you?

Direct Message to Your Lover

01If your partner was watching this right now, what would you say to them?
02What promise are you making to them today?
03Why do you choose them — today and always?

Gratitude

01Who would you like to thank as you begin this new chapter?
02What would you like to say to your parents?
03What would you like to say to your friends and everyone who stood by you?
04What does their support mean to you?
